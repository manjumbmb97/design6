About Us

I'm Manjunath from Vijayapura, India. My interests include graphic design, illustration and 3D modelling, which made me develop this project 'design6'.

Future Plans

  • Getting access to images from all the top design sites with a single search and option to save them to user's account (Premium users can edit them instantly using our online tool) or get them printed at discounted price.
  • Giving enthusiastic design bloggers a chance to contribute to our blog and earn money through it (Only for bloggers in level-5+. Total of 10 levels depending upon their post popularity, writing skills and other things). Bloggers with exceptional blogging history will be included in level-5+ automatically after verification.
  • Giving enthusiastic designers a chance to contribute to our project through their designs and earn money through it (Only for designers in level-5+. Total of 10 levels depending upon their design popularity, designing skills and other things). Exceptional designers will be included in level-5+ automatically after verification.
  • Online shop for selling t-shirts, hoodies, covers etc. from various artists across the globe at a resonable price.
  • Online tools to instantly(without user registration) apply cool effects to images or create cool text effects and save them in different formats
  • For premium users: Industry standard cloud based graphic editor and vector drawing/converter tools.
